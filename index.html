Privacy Policy

Crunchyroll Auto Skip PRO does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data.

The extension runs entirely within the user’s browser and only interacts with Crunchyroll pages to detect and control video playback.

Public APIs such as AniSkip and AniList are used solely to retrieve anime skip timestamps and metadata. No personally identifiable information, browsing history, or user activity is collected or shared.

All settings (such as enabling or disabling auto skip) are stored locally using Chrome’s storage API.
